About TRACESCFEED

Welcome to TRACESCFEED, the official platform for students of TRACE College to express their thoughts, share ideas, and raise concerns in a constructive and respectful manner. Our mission is to foster a stronger, more connected campus community by giving every student a voice.

How It Works

  1. Write a Post: Navigate to the "New Post" section to share what's on your mind. You can write about school policies, suggest events, give feedback on teachers, or discuss any other campus-related topic.
  2. Choose a Category: To keep discussions organized, please select the most relevant category for your post, such as Events, School, Teachers, or Staff.
  3. Review Process: To ensure all discussions are productive and adhere to community guidelines, every submission is reviewed by the Student Council before being published on the site. This helps maintain a safe and positive environment for everyone.
  4. Engage with the Community: Browse posts from fellow students, see what topics are trending, and stay informed about what's happening around campus.

TRACESCFEED is more than just a forum; it's a tool for change. Your feedback is valuable and helps the Student Council better represent your interests and work towards a better TRACE College for all.

Let your voice be heard!
